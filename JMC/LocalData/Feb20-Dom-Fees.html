FEE-HELP // FEE-HELP is available for all Higher Education Courses. JMC Academy is a registered Higher Education Provider, which means that students are able to access the Commonwealths FEE-HELP loan scheme to assist with their financial commitments during study. For more information, please contact Administration, visit our website or access the Commonwealths information service at www.studyassist.gov.au.

REGISTRATION AND ACCREDITATION // JMC Academy is a Higher Education Provider registered and accredited under the Tertiary Education Quality Standards Agency (TEQSA).
